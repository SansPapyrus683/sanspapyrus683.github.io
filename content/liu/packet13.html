significance test for \(\mu\)

same shit as always, but quick rundown here:

  1. name: 1-sample t-test for \(\mu\)
  2. conditions: RNG, 10%, & normality check
  3. test stat: \(t=\frac{\bar{x}-\mu}{\frac{s_x}{\sqrt{n}}}\)
  4. to get the area/p-val, use \(\text{tcdf}\)
